About Safe Kitchen Collective

We research material composition in modern kitchen appliances to help families make safer, healthier choices for their homes.

Our Mission

Your family deserves appliances that are genuinely safe. We strip away marketing fluff to deliver evidence-backed guidance on material purity, chemical-free cooking, and long-term health protection.

Founded in 2025, Safe Kitchen Collective emerged from a simple understanding: most families have no idea what their cookware is made of or what chemicals might be migrating into their food. We set out to change that by creating the most comprehensive, transparent resource for non-toxic kitchen appliances.

To date, we've analyzed air fryers using our proprietary 100-point safety scoring framework, read reviews and manufacturers' documentation, and look to built a community of safety-conscious families who refuse to compromise on health.

Our Core Values

Evidence-Based

Every recommendation is backed by scientific research, manufacturer documentation, and real-world testing. We don't rely on marketing claims.

Independent

We accept no sponsorships or payments from manufacturers for favorable reviews. Our rankings are 100% independent and unbiased.

Family-Focused

We prioritize the health of families — especially children — by identifying products that eliminate chemical exposure risks in the kitchen.

Educational

We empower families with knowledge, not just product recommendations. Understanding material science helps you make informed choices for years to come.

Continuous Improvement

As new research emerges and products evolve, we continuously update our reviews and scoring to reflect the latest information.

Community

We learn from our community's experiences and questions. Your feedback helps us identify new products to review and areas to research.
