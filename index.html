Why Material Purity Matters

Most air fryers still rely on non-stick coatings—silently leaching chemicals into meals. Only glass and stainless surfaces eliminate this risk entirely. Anything else is a compromise.

How to Choose the Safest Air Fryer

Following these guidelines will ensure that you choose appliances that are safe, durable, and aligned with our mission-driven standards which prioritize health over convenience, every single time.

  • Material Contact: Glass or stainless only in contact with food.
  • Eliminate Coatings: Avoid PTFE, PFOA, and ceramic non-stick.
  • Temperature Stability: Components must not degrade under high heat.
  • Certifications & Transparency: Verify documentation & third-party recognition.
  • Ease of Cleaning: Glass & stainless are easy to inspect & maintain.
