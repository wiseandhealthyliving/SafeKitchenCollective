Why Material Purity Matters

Most air fryers still rely on non-stick coatings — silently leaching chemicals into every meal. Only glass and stainless surfaces eliminate this risk entirely. Anything else is a compromise.

How to Choose the Safest Air Fryer

  • Material Contact: Glass or stainless only in contact with food.
  • Eliminate Coatings: Avoid PTFE, PFOA, ceramic, or non-stick coatings.
  • Temperature Stability: Components must withstand high heat without degrading.
  • Certifications & Transparency: Verify documentation and third-party recognition.
  • Ease of Cleaning: Glass and stainless are easy to inspect and maintain.

Following these guidelines ensures you choose appliances that are safe, durable, and aligned with our mission-driven standards.
